Co-creating the future

VORTEX-Colab is a Collaborative Laboratory that brings academia and industry in perfect sync.

We are your partner in progress

Since 2019, we have been the go-to for technology acceleration that disrupts industries like Automotive, Space, and Defense.

Our Mission?

Accelerate the launch of cutting-edge cyber-physical systems and cybersecurity solutions.

Our Vision?

Create outstanding innovations that propel society forward.

Our Purpose?

Unite the best minds from academia and industry to shape the future.

Once we bridge the gap, we seal the success.

Our process

Our process is clear and effective

  • Tailor-made solutions, from prototyping and consulting to technology training.
  • Accelerate time to market, minimizing risks and maximizing rewards.
  • Unique solutions that not only solve current problems but anticipate future challenges.
  • Flexible licensing and strategic funding options that adapt to your needs.
  • Partner with you to create not just products, but industry-shaping innovations.
